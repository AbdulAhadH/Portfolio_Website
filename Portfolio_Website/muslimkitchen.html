Summary

Muslim Kitchen App

Muslim Kitchen is an Android App released on the Google Play Store. I designed this app from scratch because it allowed me to design the app the way I personally wanted to. I ran into many problems during the duration of the project but I was able to overcome them. This project helped me to learn alot about android app development from the front-end aspect in particular. I learned the importance of sizing and margins and I learned how to allow the app to work on old and new phones.

Date: March - May, 2019



Details

Details

This project was made on Android Studio using Java and XML. The database that was used was: Firebase-Google. The app uses a splash screen, fragments, a bottom navigation view, google admob ads and firebase.
